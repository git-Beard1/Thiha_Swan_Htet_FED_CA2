| SP Efforts

SP Sustainability

Dispensers

Many water dispensers can be seen in every corner of the campus or classes. These devices allow students, and staffs to cease the use of plastic bottled water.

Flora

The large and wide campus of SP is the home to a large variety of majestic plant species including the Brown Woolly Fig Heritage Trees found near foodcourt 1.

Green Buildings

The three buildings : T18A, T18B, and the Sports Arena have been certified as Green Mark Platinum. Let us aim for all 22 buildings to get certified by 2025.

Smart Offices

Offices and classrooms are also monitored by smart sensors. A pleasant and healthy environment enhances efficiency and keeps the environment sustainable.

Sensors

Up to more than 500 smart sensors reside in SP. These smart sensors maintain the air quality and track human traffic around the campus. These systems keep us healthy.

Curriculums

Common Core Curriculums are introduced in school to prepare students for the green economy. The curriculum includes recent real world issues as well as the UNSDGs.

Solar PV Basics

In this Solar PV Basic course, participants will learn the principles of solar panel electricity production and explore the main components of solar systems. Dive deep into energy systems.

Formulation Science

This course equips the participants with the necessary knowledge in the areas of formulation science in cosmetics. Participants will learn through project learning and hands-on trainings.

Advanced PV

In this course, participants will learn the essential knowledge and skills in PV system planning, design and installation. Application of skills is done through multiple projects and trainings.

Environmental Control

The primary objective of the (ECO) course is to equip premise managers with the knowledge and skills necessary to adopt a proactive and diligent approach to ensure a healthy condition.

Sustainability Practices

This course introduces participants to have essential understanding, skills, and proficiencies for sustainable reportings in alignments with ecological guidelines and common cores.

Sustainable Energy

The course aims to drive innovative mindshare by facilitating the exchange of industrial best practices among world leaders who possessed an advanced tech equipments in sustainable energy.

Plastic Waste Pavement

Local construction Samwoh and SP researchers also collaborated to develop a new type of durable asphalts made with plastic waste. The team found that asphalt made from plastic waste could last at least 20 per cent longer than regular asphalt, and had levels of chemical leaching that were within official guidelines.
